# BrightRoute Website Self-Critique

## 1. Customer looking for service
- The hero answers the main questions quickly: mobile headlight restoration, clearer/safter headlights, call/text/quote actions.
- The before/after slider is the most convincing visual and appears above the fold.
- Pricing is simple and not overwhelming.
- Remaining friction: real phone number, city list, pricing, and genuine reviews need confirmation before launch.

## 2. Professional web designer
- Design uses a premium automotive palette based on the logo: navy, white, amber/yellow, and electric-blue accents.
- The page is mobile-first with large CTAs, sticky mobile contact bar, high-contrast sections, accessible labels, compressed WebP images, and a restrained animation footprint.
- The strongest images were used; weaker cluttered/duplicated shots were not used as primary visuals.
- Visible plates were avoided or blurred in published assets.
- Remaining improvement: add more finished after photos and a true transparent/SVG logo when available.

## 3. Local SEO consultant
- Page includes meta title/description, Open Graph tags, canonical URL, sitemap, robots.txt, LocalBusiness/AutoRepair schema, service offers, and FAQ schema.
- Copy targets headlight restoration near me, mobile headlight restoration, restore foggy headlights, headlight cleaning service, and city/county modifiers.
- Service-area copy is ready for real city/county targeting.
- Remaining improvement: replace preview phone/email/social links, add Google Business Profile link, real reviews, exact address/service area, and final domain.
