FAQ

Common questions

How long will restored headlights last?

Longevity depends on lens condition, sun exposure, and maintenance. UV protection helps the result last longer than a basic polish.

Can every headlight be restored?

Most oxidized plastic lenses can improve dramatically, but cracked lenses, internal moisture, or internal damage may require replacement.

Do I need to provide water or power?

Confirm before publishing: this depends on the final BrightRoute equipment setup. The site is prepared to explain mobile requirements clearly.

Is this better than a DIY kit?

DIY kits often skip thorough prep and UV protection. A professional process gives a cleaner, more consistent, longer-lasting result.
